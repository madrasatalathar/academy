Key instructors and brief bios

Ibrahim Abdul Adheem

Reverted: Beginning of Sha'ban 1444 AH

After a year of research following his reversion, he embraced the true Aqidah and Manhaj and chose the Maliki madhhab in fiqh due to its strong connection to Ahl al-Madinah. In Shawwal 1446 AH he founded the Madrasat Al-Athar project on YouTube and Discord to bring authentic Salafi knowledge to the youth.

Ijazat:

  • Usul as-Sunnah (Imam Ahmad)
  • Usul ath-Thalathah (Shaykh al-Islam Muhammad ibn Abdul-Wahhab)
  • Nawaqid al-Islam (Shaykh al-Islam Muhammad ibn Abdul-Wahhab)
  • Al-Qawa'id al-Arba' (Shaykh al-Islam Muhammad ibn Abdul-Wahhab)
  • Hadith Jibril (Imam al-Bukhari)
  • Thulathiyat al-Bukhari
  • Al-Ha'iyyah (Imam Ibn Abi Dawud)
  • Risalat al-Qadr ('Umar ibn al-Khattab)
