Ibrahim Abdul Adheem

He reverted on the 4th Jumada Al-Akhirah, 1427H. After two years, he began formally studying Islam and went on to receive ijazat in Usul ath-Thalathah, Al-Qawaid al-Arba, Usul as-Sunnah (Imam Ahmad), Al-Haiyyah, Risalah of Umar on Qadr, Hadith Jibril, and Thulathiyat al-Bukhari.
